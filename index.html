Your Imagination, your photo

head with this perfect app.

Bring your imagination to life with this photapp. Anything that you have in your head in terms of how you want a photo to look can be achieved right here, with a vast range of effects and custom additions you can place in the photo. Once done, you can upload to your desired site with the click of a button!

Capture

Capture the perfect image with the in-app camera mode. It comes with a host of pre-made filters if you desire to have so but also in normal mode, the app will scan everything in the frame and optimise the colours in it to give the most exuberant or natural photo, depending on your desire.

Add to

Add elements to your picture to attain the effect you desire. This can be something to add a bit of fun or personality to your image or something more lifelike, like to add a sun with the light effects that having so would give and brighten up a particular image. It can bring a whole new element to your pictures and can be great fun to use!
